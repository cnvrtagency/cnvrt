# CNVRT Google Ads service research

Research date: 16 July 2026  
Status: Current working brief for `/services/google-ads/`  
Scope: Official Google guidance and ten UK Google Ads or PPC agency propositions

## 1. Executive decision

CNVRT should position Google Ads as a commercially managed acquisition system rather than a list of campaign types.

The credible service connects:

1. Search demand and business economics.
2. Account and conversion-goal structure.
3. Search campaigns, terms, ads, budgets and exclusions.
4. Landing-page relevance and conversion journeys.
5. Qualified offline leads or transaction value where appropriate.
6. Consent-aware measurement.
7. Commercial reporting and a controlled route to scale.

Google Search is the core offer. Shopping and Performance Max are appropriate only when an ecommerce business has suitable product data, Merchant Center setup, creative assets, conversion values and enough useful signal. Microsoft Ads is separately scoped.

## 2. Current Google platform guidance

### Conversion quality

Google recommends enhanced conversions for leads as the upgraded route for importing offline lead outcomes. From April 2026, enhanced conversion settings are being unified, and the Data Manager route is central to current implementation guidance.

CNVRT should therefore discuss qualified or closed leads, transaction value and CRM feedback where the client has a lawful, reliable source of data. A basic form submission should not automatically be presented as the final business outcome.

Sources:

- [Google Ads, offline conversion imports](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2998031)
- [Google Ads, offline conversion import guidelines](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/15081888)

### Search-term control

Google's search terms report shows searches that triggered ads and recommends using less relevant searches to refine keywords or add negative keywords. Negative keywords now apply across relevant Search and Shopping inventory, including Performance Max in supported configurations.

Sources:

- [Google Ads, search terms report](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2684537)
- [Google Ads, negative keywords](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/7102995)

### Shopping and Performance Max

Merchant Center product data is used to match products to advertising opportunities and can surface product image, price, store and other information across Google channels. This makes feed quality, availability, pricing and conversion value part of campaign management rather than a separate housekeeping task.

Source: [Google Merchant Center, advertise products](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/12500202)

### Consent and first-party data

Google requires consent signals for relevant EEA measurement and personalisation use cases. Consent mode includes `ad_storage`, `ad_user_data`, `ad_personalization` and `analytics_storage` states. This is a measurement and privacy dependency, not a box the ad manager should silently bypass.

Source: [Google Ads, consent mode reference](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/13802165)

### Account ownership

Google states that a client account retains its data and can remove manager access by unlinking. CNVRT should therefore manage accounts through appropriate access while clients retain administrative access, billing control and their data.

Source: [Google Ads, manager account ownership](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/7456532)

### Google Partner status

Google currently lists performance, spend and certification requirements for Partner status. The published Partner thresholds include a 70 percent optimisation score, USD 10,000 in managed 90-day spend and relevant certification coverage. CNVRT must not use a Partner badge until status is shown as achieved in the registered manager account.

Source: [Google Ads, Google Partner requirements](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/9702452)

## 4. Recurring competitor strengths

- Free audit or review as a low-friction entry point.
- Google Partner or Premier Partner credentials where earned.
- Search, Shopping, Performance Max and remarketing coverage.
- Case studies with CPA, ROAS, revenue or conversion evidence.
- Transparent reporting and named review cadence.
- Landing-page and conversion-rate work alongside account management.
- Ecommerce product-feed and Merchant Center expertise.
- Clear pricing or minimum budget on some specialist pages.
- Direct access to a specialist or named founder.

## 5. Recurring competitor weaknesses

- Claiming to maximise ROI without explaining margin, lead quality or attribution.
- Treating every campaign type as automatically suitable.
- Presenting platform automation as either magic or something that should be entirely resisted.
- Reporting form fills as equal conversions without offline qualification.
- Limited discussion of consent, account ownership and data access.
- Heavy superlatives and award language with thin delivery detail.
- Long generic service lists with little prioritisation.
- Weak explanation of what is excluded or separately scoped.

## 6. Approved CNVRT service boundary

### Core Google Ads management

- Account, access and history audit.
- Demand, competition and economics review.
- Conversion-goal and tracking audit.
- Google Search campaign strategy, creation and management.
- Keywords, match types, search terms and negative keywords.
- Ad copy and relevant Google Ads assets.
- Location, schedule, device, network, budget and bidding decisions.
- Landing-page review and prioritised recommendations.
- Qualified-lead or sales-value feedback where feasible.
- Commercial reporting and next-action explanation.

### Conditional or separately scoped

- Merchant Center, Shopping and Performance Max for suitable ecommerce accounts.
- Feed repair or product-data enrichment where workload is material.
- Display, YouTube, Demand Gen, App and substantial creative production.
- New landing-page design and development.
- CRM or Data Manager integrations.
- Call-tracking subscriptions or other third-party tools.
- Microsoft Ads management.

### Not promised

- A guaranteed number of leads, sales, ROAS or CPA.
- Guaranteed approval of ads, products or accounts.
- A Google Partner badge before Google confirms eligibility.
- Blind adoption of platform recommendations.
- Management through an account the client cannot access.
- Tracking or remarketing that bypasses consent requirements.

## 7. Page decisions implemented

- Primary search role changed to `Google Ads agency Manchester` and `PPC management UK`.
- Search is now the clear core service.
- Automation is framed around the quality of conversion signals and commercial outcomes.
- Outcome and scope cards now use longer, equally weighted copy.
- Shopping and Performance Max are conditional on product data, Merchant Center, creative, value tracking and campaign fit.
- Microsoft Ads is explicitly separately scoped.
- Client account ownership, consent and offline lead-quality feedback are covered in the FAQ and delivery scope.
- Related services now connect landing pages, SEO demand and CRM outcomes to the paid-search programme.
- Hear Better remains the proof project, but its exact 10x ROAS reporting period and source remain open in the evidence register.
