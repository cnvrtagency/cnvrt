## Homepage change boundary

Daniel has confirmed that the existing homepage is liked and should not change substantially.

The homepage work is therefore an editorial refinement, not a redesign or structural rewrite:

- Preserve the current section order and overall design rhythm.
- Preserve the existing imagery, interactions, motion and established visual treatments unless a specific issue is approved for correction.
- Preserve the selected-client marquee, selected-work presentation, service rows, engagement routes, customer journeys, process, audit, articles, FAQs and contact form.
- Refine copy only where it improves agency positioning, service balance, accuracy, SEO or conversion clarity.
- Do not replace existing approved headings merely because an alternative appears in this copy deck.
- Treat the proposed copy below as options to compare against the current page, not an instruction to replace every line.
- Any material layout or structural change requires separate approval.

## Copy principles

- The homepage owns the broad “digital marketing agency Manchester” topic.
- Web Design remains a lead specialism but does not dominate the homepage.
- The copy explains commercial problems before listing deliverables.
- Claims remain specific, credible and evidence-led.
- Headings are written for controlled editorial line breaks, but final line balance must be validated in the actual layouts at every target width.
- No invented statistics, awards, team language or platform accreditations appear.

### FAQs

#### What does CNVRT do?

> CNVRT is a Manchester digital marketing agency providing web design, ecommerce, SEO, AEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, email marketing and custom development. Services can be commissioned individually or connected around a wider commercial goal.

#### What types of business does CNVRT work with?

> CNVRT works with ecommerce brands, service businesses, local companies and funded startups. The important factors are a credible commercial opportunity, realistic investment and the ability to act on the work.

#### Do I need a new website before starting marketing?

> Not always. An existing website may support focused search, advertising or email work. If it is limiting performance, CNVRT will explain what needs to change, why it matters and whether improvement or replacement is more sensible.

#### Can CNVRT manage several channels together?

> Yes. Websites, search, paid media, email and measurement can be managed as one connected programme or introduced in stages around the most important opportunity.

#### Does CNVRT work outside Manchester?

> Yes. CNVRT is based in Manchester and works with businesses across the UK and beyond through a clear remote process.

#### How much does an engagement cost?

> Focused projects start from £1,000. Ongoing work is scoped around the services, workload, business size, media spend and reporting requirements. Ongoing engagements begin with a three-month minimum.

#### Can I request an audit first?

> Yes. CNVRT offers free, qualification-led audits where there is enough information to provide useful direction. Audits can cover websites, ecommerce, paid media, email, SEO or AEO.
