## 1. Purpose of this document

This document converts Daniel's answers to date into a page-by-page website and content strategy. It is the decision layer between research and implementation.

Nothing in this document authorises an automatic website change. Recommendations are labelled as recommendations, unresolved facts are labelled as evidence required, and implementation only begins after approval.

## 2. Confirmed commercial position

### What CNVRT is

CNVRT is a Manchester digital marketing and growth agency working with businesses across the UK and beyond.

CNVRT connects websites, ecommerce, search, paid acquisition, email and useful custom technology around the commercial problem currently limiting growth.

Web design is the lead specialism, but the homepage must present CNVRT as a digital marketing agency. It must not behave like a duplicate Web Design service page.

### What makes CNVRT different

- Connected, multi-channel thinking rather than isolated channel delivery.
- Direct involvement and faster decisions.
- Work is prioritised around commercial need, not simply hours allocated.
- Practical support for founders and businesses without an experienced internal digital function.
- Capability spanning acquisition, conversion, retention and operational tools.
- Honest recommendations without unrealistic guarantees.

### Priority audiences

- Ecommerce brands.
- Service businesses.
- Local businesses.
- Funded startups and new businesses with a realistic launch budget.
- Established small businesses through to enterprise organisations.

CNVRT should qualify by commercial fit, ambition and ability to invest, not by company-size labels alone.

### Confirmed commercial boundaries

- Focused projects start from £1,000.
- Typical project delivery is two to eight weeks, depending on scope.
- Ongoing engagements have a three-month minimum so there is enough time to implement, measure and improve.
- Ongoing pricing depends on service, business size, workload and commercial context.
- Aftercare, hosting, support and training can be scoped where needed.
- Audits are free and qualification-led, with a target response within one working day.
- Contact is through forms and email. A custom booking route may be built later.

### Presentation rules

- Do not claim or imply a team.
- Do not present CNVRT as a lone freelancer either.
- Daniel Lyons can be visible as the owner and accountable expert once his biography and preferred level of visibility are approved.
- Do not claim a Manchester office, address or Google Business Profile until there is a legitimate, policy-compliant basis.
- Do not publish guarantees, invented outcomes or unverified badges.
- Do not overemphasise frameworks such as Next.js. Explain the commercial purpose of the technology.

## 5. Page-by-page content briefs

## Homepage

**Job:** Establish CNVRT as a credible Manchester digital marketing agency, communicate the connected-growth proposition, show proof quickly and route different buyers to the right next step.

**Must communicate:**

- Category and Manchester relevance in the H1.
- Web design as a lead strength without allowing it to dominate the page.
- The full connected service set at comparable visual priority.
- The problems CNVRT solves: fragmented channels, slow delivery, weak ownership, poor value, unclear priorities and ineffective acquisition.
- Proof through selected clients, case studies and carefully defined figures.
- Three clear paths: book a conversation, submit a project and request an audit.

**Recommended section order:**

1. Category-led hero with one primary action and one secondary action.
2. Concise proof strip using only verified claims and client logos with permission.
3. Connected services in editorial rows, not a web-design-heavy card hierarchy.
4. Featured Hear Better case study as joined-up growth proof.
5. Two additional selected-work entries, initially TopDraw and UberKinky or Heatons.
6. How CNVRT works, using four concise steps.
7. Engagement routes: focused project, ongoing growth and audit.
8. Founder-led trust or testimonial proof.
9. Useful guides selected for buyer intent, not filler blog posts.
10. Contact form and final CTA.

**Do not:** Copy the detailed Web Design page, give every service a separate keyword paragraph, or lead with vague creative language that fails to identify the agency.

**Approved change boundary:** Preserve the current homepage structure, design rhythm, imagery, interactions and article section. Improve it through selective copy refinement and service balancing rather than a substantial redesign. Existing headings remain unless a specific replacement is approved. Placeholder article cards must be replaced with complete guides before launch, not removed from the design.

## AEO

**Intent:** AEO agency UK and AI search optimisation services for organisations that need to understand and improve how they are represented, mentioned or cited in AI-led answers.

**Must cover:** A documented visibility baseline, commercially relevant prompts, entity clarity, evidence, authorship, answer-led content, source quality, crawlability, supported structured data, repeat monitoring, AI referrals and qualified outcomes.

**Monitoring method:** Record the prompt, platform, available mode or model detail, date and UK market context. Review mentions, citations, cited pages, competitors and source patterns. Connect this directional data to Bing AI Performance where available, first-party referral traffic, landing pages and qualified actions.

**Truth requirement:** Do not imply guaranteed inclusion in an AI answer or present a changing prompt response as a fixed ranking. Google states that its AI features use existing SEO best practices and require no special AI schema or text file. AEO has a distinct buyer intent and measurement emphasis but shares crawlability, indexation, useful content and authority foundations with SEO.

**Proof:** Hear Better is the relevant content-system example. No AEO result should be claimed until platform-level citation evidence is documented and approved.

**Research:** The official-platform and ten-agency review is recorded in `docs/CNVRT-AEO-COMPETITOR-RESEARCH.md`.

**Separately scoped:** Platform migrations, SMS, WhatsApp, push and other messaging channels, transactional communications, complex integrations or custom events, localisation, new photography, extensive custom illustration or motion, unusual email code and production beyond the agreed allowance.
