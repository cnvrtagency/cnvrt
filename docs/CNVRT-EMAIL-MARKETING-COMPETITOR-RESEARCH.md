# CNVRT Email Marketing competitor and service-page research

Updated: 16 July 2026  
Status: Approved working direction for `/services/email-marketing/`

## Executive decision

CNVRT should present Email Marketing as a permission-based lifecycle and retention service, not as a newsletter production service.

The commercial page should connect customer data, campaigns, automated flows, segmentation, onsite capture, deliverability, creative, testing and reporting. Klaviyo is the preferred ecommerce platform because of its Shopify data and event depth. Mailchimp remains supported for suitable existing accounts, service businesses and simpler programmes.

A separate Klaviyo landing page should not be published yet. Build it only when CNVRT has verified Heatons email evidence, at least one additional documented Klaviyo delivery example and enough platform depth or current partner status to make the page materially more useful than the Email Marketing hub.

## Official platform findings

### Klaviyo

- Flows are automated actions triggered by customer behaviour, list activity, dates or operational events. The service copy should therefore explain triggers, filters and exit logic rather than treating every automation as an abandoned-cart sequence.
- Shopify can supply customer, product, order and behavioural events that support deeper segmentation and lifecycle journeys.
- Sender authentication, branded sending domains, suppression, bounces and list cleaning affect sender reputation and deliverability. No agency can guarantee inbox placement.
- Klaviyo now presents itself as a wider B2C CRM across email and additional messaging channels. Email management must not silently imply that SMS, WhatsApp, push or RCS production is included.
- Klaviyo's strategic services cover onboarding, migrations, integrations, deliverability, warming, segmentation and campaign planning. These provide a useful benchmark for a credible agency scope.
- K:Partners is a formal agency programme. CNVRT must not display partner status or a partner badge until acceptance and the current rights to use those assets are verified.

### Mailchimp

- Mailchimp can connect Shopify customer, order and behavioural data, so supporting Mailchimp does not mean limiting the service to batch newsletters.
- Express, verifiable permission and retained consent records matter. Purchased, scraped or unexplained lists should not be accepted.
- Email authentication and sender verification are different. Authentication work should be described precisely.
- Site tracking requires the advertiser to manage an appropriate consent route.
- Mailchimp attribution uses defined windows and a last-touch model. It should be read alongside wider business evidence rather than presented as complete commercial truth.
- Apple Mail Privacy Protection and automated activity can inflate open and click reporting. Opens should not be a primary success measure.
- Mailchimp & Co is free to join at Member level, while the Partner badge requires current account and training thresholds. CNVRT should not claim Partner status before the dashboard confirms it.

## Market strengths to retain

- Lifecycle and retention framing.
- Visible platform and ecommerce experience.
- A clear audit or setup route before ongoing management.
- Campaigns and flows treated as connected but different work.
- Copy, design, build, testing and reporting included where appropriate.
- Deliverability, list health and permission treated as core operating concerns.
- A defined monthly production allowance or package boundary.

## Common weaknesses CNVRT can avoid

- Claiming email-attributed revenue without explaining the platform window or attribution limits.
- Using partner badges as the main evidence of strategic ability.
- Leaving ownership of the platform, billing and customer data unclear.
- Bundling SMS, transactional messages or migrations into a generic email-management promise.
- Treating creative production as unlimited.
- Listing flows without explaining prioritisation, triggers, filters or customer purpose.
- Reporting open rate as a decisive measure despite privacy and automated-activity distortion.
- Ignoring the landing page, storefront, CRM or paid-acquisition context surrounding the email programme.

## Approved CNVRT service boundary

### Core service

- Account, lifecycle and data audit.
- Campaign strategy, calendar and agreed production allowance.
- Copy, responsive design, platform build and quality assurance.
- Automated-flow planning and implementation.
- Segmentation and onsite permissioned capture.
- Shopify or suitable CRM integration using available standard data.
- Sender-domain, authentication, suppression and list-health review.
- Testing, commercial reporting and prioritised improvement.

### Separately assessed and scoped

- Platform migration and cutover.
- SMS, WhatsApp, push, RCS or other messaging channels.
- Transactional and operational notifications.
- Complex integrations, custom events or data engineering.
- New photography, extensive custom illustration, complex motion or unusual email code.
- Localisation and high-volume content production beyond the agreed allowance.

### Operating safeguards

- The client retains ownership, billing control and administrative access to the platform and customer data.
- CNVRT uses an appropriate user or partner role rather than shared personal credentials.
- Purchased, scraped or unexplained lists are not accepted as a growth shortcut.
- Inbox placement, revenue and platform-attributed performance are not guaranteed.
- Consent and legal compliance remain a shared operational responsibility and require qualified legal advice where the position is uncertain.

## Approved page decisions

- Target `email marketing agency Manchester` naturally in the title, description and eyebrow.
- Lead with customer behaviour, permission and lifecycle value rather than send volume.
- Use Klaviyo-preferred and Mailchimp-supported wording with a clear selection rationale.
- Give campaigns, flows, capture, deliverability, segmentation and reporting comparable visibility.
- Keep the Heatons example qualitative until source records, attribution, date range and Tony Keigher's approval are complete.
- Route visitors to Shopify, Meta Ads and Custom Tools to show how email connects to the wider customer system.
- Use FAQs to make production, migration, channel, ownership and deliverability boundaries explicit.
